To calculate the pace of a run, press the Calculate Pace button. This will open the Calculate Pace Window.

To Calculate the Pace of Your Run:
1) Enter your runtime in the respective Hours, Minutes and Seconds fields. The seconds can be entered in decimal format.
2) Enter the run distance in the Distance field.
Like the seconds value, the distance can be entered in decimal format.
3) Press the Calculate Pace button.

Press the Clear button to clear the data entered.

Press the Cancel button to cancel the calculation.

To save the data, press the Save button.

You can change the units by selecting Miles, Kilometers or Meters radio button.

NOTE: In order to calculate the pace, data must be entered in the Distance, Minutes and Seconds fields.
