To Calculate the Runtime:
1) Enter the Distance of your run. This can be entered in decimal format.
2) Enter the Pace of your run in Minutes and Seconds.
Like the Distance field, the seconds can be entered in decimal format.
3) Press the Calculate Run Time button.

Press the Clear button to clear the data entered.

Press the Cancel button to cancel the calculation.

To save the data, press the Save button.

You can change the units by selecting Miles, Kilometers or Meters radio button.

NOTE: In order to calculate the pace, data must be entered in all three text fields.
